About

Hi! I am a self taught developer currently working as an Integration Consultant for Workday. I work on customer deployments large and small, implementing our Human Capital Management product among others. Originally a Political Science major in my undergrad years, I discovered a love for coding while taking a couple java elective courses. Wanting to speed up my learning, I began freelancing building websites before heading off to learn web development at Le Wagon in Berlin in the summer of 2018. I currently work with a variety of technologies including XSLT and Workday Studio to build integrations for customers within a vast variety of requirements.

Career

Beginning working for Workday in the Summer of 2019 as an Associate IC, I quickly became certified in Workday HCM, Integration Core, Studio, Payroll Interface. Over the course of the year I worked on deployments for 8+ different customers, on integrations ranging from custom studio integrations to a college transcript report design. In the fall of 2020 I was promoted to Senior Associate IC as the team approached several customer go-lives at the end of the year. I am currently leading my first project in the ME (Medium Enterprise) space, designing, building, and managing the development and deployement of benefits and payroll integrations in scope.
